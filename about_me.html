About me

Welcome, I hope that you are having a fantastic day.

I'm Wesley, a student at 42 Silicon Valley looking for a career in software development.

My interest in software sparked when I was an accountant working at Deloitte. While I was studying for the CPA exams, I realize that the tools we were using at work interested me much more than the accounting theories.

After receiving my CPA license, the two choices I gave myself was to continue my career as an accountant or start from the beginning and dive into programming. Since I made my decision I have yet to look back.
